How do you ask a question on an Instagram story?

If you are new to the app and want to get social and engage with your followers through questions on your stories, then here is how to go about it:

  • Launch the Instagram app on your device.
  • Click on the "Your Story" option on the upper left. Take a photo or select one from your gallery by swiping it up.
  • Select the picture that forms the background image of the question tag.
  • Tap on the icon that looks like a square smiley face. This option will lead you to the features you can use on Instagram stories.
  • Find the "Questions" in the options. Tap in the field that says, "Ask me a question". This is the default option. If you wish to customize your question, tap the text field. If you are looking for music suggestions, tap on the music icon next to the "Aa" option.
