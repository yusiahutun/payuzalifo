Hailey Baldwin and Justin Biebers Full Relationship Timeline

The love story between the former Hailey Baldwin and Justin Bieber is a pure testament to manifestation, as Hailey has proven it’s possible to not only date but also ultimately marry your childhood celebrity crush.

Hailey first met Justin backstage at one of his concerts, when she was a teenage fan. In a gradually progressing timeline spanning more than a decade, Justin and Hailey have evolved from parasocials to real friends, to courters, to full-blown lovers—and finally, to husband and wife.

Now, the wedded duo are gracing carpets and magazine covers with their love, and blessing the internet with ongoing bouts of PDA. To find out how Hailey went from Belieber to Mrs. Bieber, see all the latest updates surrounding the model’s friendship-turned-romance with Justin, and the duo’s now four-year-and-counting marriage below.

April 29, 2024: Hailey supports Justin during a rough patch.

After Justin shared a selfie of himself crying to social media, a source tells Entertainment Tonight that the singer is facing a hard time: “Justin has been facing some difficulties lately. He has been having a hard time and hasn’t been feeling like his usual self.”

Despite this, Hailey is lending her support to her husband. “Hailey has been doing her best to be there for Justin, but it is upsetting for her to see him struggle,” the insider says. “They are doing their best to make things work and be supportive of each other. They are committed to doing whatever it takes to make things better.”

May 18, 2023: They take a trip to London.

Justin accompanies Hailey to London, where the two celebrate her skincare brand’s official launch in the U.K.

In a carousel post on Instagram, the model poses in several solo shots featuring looks from the trip, including a silver sequin strapless gown and a floral leather co-ord. She also includes a photo of herself sharing a sweet kiss with Justin.

“London✅ you were too good to me,” she writes in the caption. “Thank you for helping me celebrate.”

During the London trip, the two are also seen on a date night at Chiltern Firehouse on May 16. For the outing, Hailey wears a black bodycon dress featuring an inkblot-shaped bustier, paired with black tights and black pointed-toe heels. Justin opts for a large black leather jacket and weathered gray hoodie over a white T-shirt and baggy light-wash jeans, topped with white Nike sneakers.

April 16: 2023: Hailey comforts Justin at Coachella.

A video of Hailey seemingly comforting her husband at Coachella has gone viral. In the clip, Justin is seen hunched over on a metal railing during the Kid Laroi’s show on day 2 of the festival, as Hailey rubs his back.

(It’s unclear what was going on in Justin’s mind at the moment, but his recent social media post suggests he may have simply been overcome with emotion.)

On day 3 of the festival, Justin shares a post on Instagram about Frank Ocean’s performance. “I was blown away by Frank Oceans Coachella performance. His artistry is simply unmatched, his style, his taste, his voice, his attention to detail.. I was deeply moved. It made me want to keep going and get better as an artist. He continues to set the bar high and gave me a night I will never forget! Thanks Frank,” he writes.

March 14, 2023: Justin shares a photo of them together.

The singer shares a photo on Instagram of him touching wife Hailey’s chin as she leans in to give him a kiss at Vanity Fair’s Oscars 2023 after-party.

March 1, 2023: Hailey celebrates Justin on his birthday.

Hailey commemorates Justin’s 29th birthday on Instagram with a carousel post featuring a series of sweet PDA photos alongside a heartfelt caption.

“29 never looked so good ☺️Happy Birthday to you best friend! Words couldn’t possibly sum up all that you embody. So here’s to more joy, more travel, yummy food, adventures, more peace, fun, and most of all more love. ✨🥳,” the model captions the post.

February 2023: They vacation in Hawaii.

Hailey and Justin take another tropical vacation—this time to Honolulu—with friends, including singer Justine Skye and model Fai Khadra, to celebrate the 30th birthday of pro surfer Kelia Moniz, a new friend and collaborator of Hailey’s.

Both share highlights from the trip on Instagram.

Later in the month, Justin leads an interview with Hailey for a cover story in which the model gets heartfelt about her relationship with Justin.

“My favorite thing about being married is honestly the companionship I feel we have,” Hailey tells her husband. “Genuinely, you are my best friend in the entire world. There’s nobody I would rather spend more time with or do anything with.”

November 22, 2022: They celebrate Hailey's birthday in Japan.

Hailey turns 26 years old while on a trip with Justin to Japan. On her official birthday in Japan Standard Time, Justin dedicates a sweet post to Hailey on Instagram with a carousel featuring their intimate moments from the trip, including multiple snapshots of the two affectionately posing together amid a thicket of bamboo trees.

"HAPPY BIRFDAY (in Japan) TO MY FAVORITE HUMAN BEING, XOXO," he writes on the post. "YOU MAKE LIFE MAGIC 😭 OBSESSED WITH EVERYTHING ABOUT YOU. LOVE YOU BUM BUM."

Hours later, Justin shares another carousel featuring silly selfies of him and Hailey on the plane, while presumably on the way back from Japan.

September 28, 2022: Hailey addresses controversies related to Justin's former romance with Selena Gomez.

For the first time, Hailey publicly clarifies the timeline of her relationship with the singer and his on-and-off-again romance with Selena Gomez, confirming the two never overlapped.

Speaking in an episode of the Call Her Daddy podcast, Hailey tells host Alexandra Cooper that Justin had already "closed a chapter" with Selena by the time she got back together with him in 2018, and adds that she would have never started things again with the singer had she not been sure that things were officially over between them.

"I understand, again, how it looks from the outside, and there's a lot of perception there," she says. "But that was a situation where I know for a fact that it was the right thing for them to close that door, they were not in a relationship at that time."

She adds, "Of course, there's a very long history there, and it's not my relationship. It has nothing to do with me, so I respect that a lot, but I know that it closed a chapter. And I think it was the best thing that could have happened for him to move on and be engaged and get married and move on with his life in that way."

Hailey also addresses her relationship critics, particularly in light of public-generated controversies surrounding how quickly she and Justin wed following the singer's final split from Selena.

"A lot of the hate and the perpetuation comes from like, 'Oh, you stole [Justin],'" she says. "I guess maybe that just comes from the fact that they wish he ended up with someone else, and that's fine. You can wish that all you want, but that's just not the case."

September 13, 2022: They celebrate their fourth wedding anniversary.

Four years after legally tying the knot in the church, the two commemorate their significant milestone in marriage with respective posts on Instagram in which they express their love and gratitude for one another.

"Happy anniversary to my best friend and wifey @haileybieber .. thanks for making me better in every way," Justin writes on Instagram.

"4 years married to you," Hailey captions a carousel post featuring sweet moments with Justin from over the years that leads with a snapshot of the two from their wedding. "The most beautiful human I've ever known... love of my life. Thank God for you. 🤍🥂."

"Happy anniversary to my best friend and wifey @haileybieber," Justin also writes on Instagram. "Thanks for making me better in every way."

August 16, 2022: Hailey calls Justin her best friend.

Speaking for Harper's BAZAAR's September 2022 cover story, the supermodel opens up about life with husband Justin Bieber and the ways in which the boundaries of their relationship have already been tested, four years in.

"I just think life is changing all the time," she says. "Day to day, week to week, year to year. I think a perfect example of that is over the last six months, both of us have gone through very serious health issues. You have to figure out how to deal with this shit as it comes, you know? There's a reason they say 'for better or for worse.' Like, that's for real!"

Recovering from recent health struggles together has made their relationship even stronger. "He's still the person that I wanna be rushing back to," Hailey says. "I might fly somewhere and go do a job, but I can't wait to come back and hang out. And I feel like that's because of the effort that's been put in on both sides."

She adds, "At the end of the day, like, he's my best friend, but it still does take a lot of work to make it work. And then I know eventually, when kids come in the picture, that's going to be a whole other season of navigating how to make that work."

August 1, 2022: Hailey praises Justin as he resumes his world tour.

The model shares a video of her husband's performance at the Lucca Summer Festival in Italy, the first show of his resumed Justice World Tour. She writes on the Instagram story, "One thing I know for certain is you can't keep this guy down... 😭 🤍."

Hailey Bieber//Instagram

July 7, 2022: They have a lakefront vacation in Idaho.

The pair enjoy a summer getaway at Idaho's Lake Coeur d'Alene following their back-to-back health scares, where they are spotted coordinating in brightly colored swimwear and sharing a kiss on a yacht outing.

June 15, 2022: Hailey supports Justin amid his diagnosis with Ramsay Hunt syndrome.

A source tells People that the couple are "unbreakable" following his diagnosis with Ramsay Hunt syndrome, a rare neurological condition that caused half of his face to be temporarily paralyzed. "Between Hailey's health scare earlier this year and now this, it's a lot for them. Hailey is worried as well," the insider says. "Just like Justin did everything he could to take care of Hailey, she now does the same for him. They are a great team and will get through it together."

Hailey also assures the public that Justin is "getting better every day" during her appearance on Live with Kelly and Ryan, saying that she and Justin are "closer than ever."

"I think honestly the silver lining in what I went through, what he went through, is it really bonds you," she says.

April 3, 2022: The two appear at the 64th Annual Grammy Awards.

Hailey, now fully recovered, makes her next red-carpet appearance alongside Justin at the 64th Annual Grammy Awards.

Axelle/Bauer-Griffin//Getty Images

March 14, 2022: Justin supports Hailey amid her blood clot scare.

A source tells People that Justin is "very worried" for Hailey after she's hospitalized for a blood clot in her brain, and that he's been there to support her in any way he can. "Hailey doesn’t want anyone to worry about her, but it was a terrifying situation for both of them," the source explains. "Justin is still very worried. He can barely sleep. He stays up watching her while she rests."

Days later, the singer addresses the situation during a show in Denver. "Most of you probably know or have seen the news about my wife," he says to the audience in between songs, "but she's okay, she's good, she's strong. It’s been scary, you know, it’s been really scary. But I know for a fact that God has her in the palms of his hands, and that’s a good thing."

He adds, "It’s kind of crazy how life randomly throws you curveballs. You can’t really control much."

March 1, 2022: Hailey gets heartfelt on Justin's birthday.

The model shares a loving, mushy Instagram post for Justin's 28th birthday. "Happy birthday my baby," she captions a series of sweet photos of the two of them. "There are a lot of amazing and beautiful things about this life, but the most beautiful part is that I get to live it with you. I love you.. here's to 28 🥳❤️."

December 11, 2021: They head to a tropical destination.

The two enjoy a weeklong tropical getaway with Hailey's longtime pal Justine Skye. In a photo dump on Instagram, Hailey poses on the beach in both a purple string bikini and cute braided hairstyle, and shares sweet moments with Skye in a matching set of headphones. Of course, she also packs on PDA with her husband.

November 1, 2021: They open up about relationship challenges.

Hailey and Justin get candid in an interview on In Good Faith With Chelsea & Judah Smith podcast about Justin's mental health and the challenges in their relationship. "I do feel like we had a lot of support," Hailey says. "I feel like if I didn’t have support, it would’ve been 10 times harder, and it was already the hardest thing in my life at the time so I also just think I was in it. I made a decision. I know for a fact that I’ve loved this person for a very long time and now would not be the time to give up on him. I just wouldn’t do that to him."

September 13, 2021: They shine at the Met Gala.

The two take their first Met Gala as a married couple in coordinated, sleek black ensembles—Hailey in a strapless Saint Laurent gown and Justin in a matching tux and white shoes.

September 12, 2021: Hailey cheers on Justin at the VMAs.

The model is her husband's ultimate supporter during his 2021 VMA performance, as she stands backstage to cheer him on. She wears an elegant cream-colored turtleneck Alaïa gown paired with an equally elegant updo.

April 13, 2021: Justin opens up about marriage struggles with Hailey.

In a new GQ cover interview, Justin talks about the struggles he experienced throughout his first year of marriage with Hailey. "It was really tough because there was a lot going back to the trauma. There was just a lack of trust…there was all these things that you don’t want to admit to the person that you’re with, because it’s scary. You don’t want to scare them off by saying, 'I’m scared,'" he said.

Now, however, the two are focusing on "creating these moments for us as a couple, as a family, that we’re building these memories," and "it’s beautiful that we have that to look forward to. Before, I didn’t have that to look forward to in my life. My home life was unstable. I didn’t have a significant other. I didn’t have someone to love. I didn’t have someone to pour into."

September 30, 2020: They celebrate their first wedding anniversary.

"1 year ago we had the best wedding," Hailey writes on Instagram. "Wish I could live this day over and over 🤍🕊."

March 1, 2020: Hailey commemorates Justin's birthday.

The model shares a series of sweet PDA photos on Instagram for Justin's 26th birthday. "Happy birthday best friend," she captions the photos. "Thank you for putting a smile on my face every single day.. I love you."

February 14, 2020: They dress up for Valentine's Day.

The two pose for this sweet Valentine's Day shoot.

December 25, 2019: They celebrate Christmas.

The two celebrate their first Christmas together as a married couple. Hailey shares sweet Polaroids from their night on Instagram, writing in the caption, "Merry Christmas from me and mine to you and yours ❤️."

October 9, 2019: They model as a married couple for Calvin Klein.

The newlyweds celebrate their marriage with a steamy NSFW Calvin Klein shoot.

September 30, 2019: They finally host their wedding.

After months of postponement, the two finally say "I do" to one another at the altar in September, a year after they also legally married.

The sweet wedding ceremony took place at Montage Palmetto Bluff, a luxury resort in Bluffton, South Carolina. According to an E! News source, "Guests arrived by boat to the rehearsal dinner on the Palmetto property. There were several water taxis that took guests over around 6:30 p.m. The dinner was on the edge of the water. There were many string lights hanging and lit candles. The dècor was very chic and white."

September 14, 2019: Hailey shuts down the doubters.

Hailey gets candid about the criticism surrounding her high-profile marriage with Justin and the trajectory of their relationship in a new cover interview. "Nobody from the outside really knows what’s going on between us," the model says. "When I had hundreds of thousands of comments bombarding me with: 'He’s never going to this,' 'You’re never going to that,' 'You’ll be divorced by then,' you can’t help but wonder: Does someone see something that I don’t see? ... But the only two people in a relationship are the two people in it."

"Look, marriage is always going to be hard and I think good relationships are the relationships that you put the work into," she also says. "I had never lived with someone before. I never had to cohabit with somebody in that way, so I was learning how to share space with someone for the first time. We were trying to bend in each other’s direction and learn what was comfortable."

April 8, 2019: Justin dedicates a poem to Hailey on Instagram.

In a total Pisces move, Justin expresses his deep love for his wife in the form of a lyrical poem.

April 1, 2019: They prank everyone on April Fools' Day.

Hailey fakes a pregnancy with Justin in an elaborate April Fools' prank involving an actual trip to the hospital. In response to some backlash over the prank's alleged insensitivity, Justin writes on Instagram, "There’s always gonna be people offended, there’s also people who don’t take jokes very well, I am a prankster and it was APRIL FOOLS'. I didn’t at all mean to be insensitive to people who can’t have children."

March 22, 2019: They buy a home in Beverly Hills.

The couple splurge on a lovely 6,132-square-foot home in one of the most expensive areas of Los Angeles.

March 19, 2019: They postpone their wedding.

Following their original February 28 save the dates, the couple ultimately push back their wedding date to give Justin time to focus on his mental health. "They will still have a wedding eventually, but this is not their focus right now," a source tells People. "They haven't sent out any new save the dates. They will wait until Justin feels better and is excited about planning their wedding again."

November 22, 2018: They spend Thanksgiving together with family.

The two celebrate the holidays together with their families at their new 9,000-square-foot lake house in Waterloo, Ontario. "First Thanksgiving as a married man, first time hosting Thanksgiving," Justin writes on Instagram. "First time both sides of the family has come together. Relationships are hard and love isn't always easy but thank you Jesus for showing me how!"

September 17, 2018: Sources confirm that they're married.

Following swirling rumors that the two got married, Hailey's uncle Alec Baldwin confirms that the two are indeed married. "They went off and got married," Alec reveals to Access on the Emmys red carpet. "I don't know what the deal is!"

"They got legally married, but [Hailey] believes marriage between God is the real thing," an insider also tells Us Weekly.

July 7, 2018: They get engaged in the Bahamas.

Two eyewitnesses saw their engagement, per TMZ. "Everyone was at a restaurant on the property doing salsa dancing when Justin’s security told everyone to put their phones away, because something special was about to happen," the site reports. "Justin then proposed in front of everyone."

Justin later confirms the engagement with a lengthy, heartfelt Instagram post, writing in part of the caption, "Hailey I am soooo in love with everything about you! So committed to spending my life getting to know every single part of you loving you patiently and kindLY."

June 29, 2018: A source confirms that they're dating again.

After several more times in which they were spotted getting steamy, a source confirms to Entertainment Tonight that the two are indeed back together.

June 10, 2018: The two rekindle in Miami.

Justin and Hailey make amends after bumping into each other at a church conference in Miami. "They love to party and they love their church organization," a source tells E!, adding that the two have "finally cleared the air and rekindled their friendship, and realized they genuinely have a lot of fun together."

Hailey later spills the details of the run-in with Justin to ELLE. "I remember we were hanging out and I was like, 'Listen, I’m really, really happy for us to be friends again. I want us to always be cool and be friends,'" she recalls. "He was like, 'Yeah, here's the thing: We're not going to be friends.' And I was like, 'Oh. Is that so?' … I think we were both a little unsure about what was going on in the very beginning. It was familiar territory, but when a lot of time goes by, it's like getting to know a new person."

August 15, 2016: Justin unfollows Hailey on Instagram.

The two face a riff in their relationship and enter a dark period lasting several years, during which they split up and are no longer friends. "Justin unfollowed Hailey on social media," a source tells People, adding, "Bieber wasn't happy about [her friendship with Drake] and so they haven't really been in touch in a bit." Meanwhile, Justin gets back together with Selena Gomez from late 2017 to May 2018.

"There was a time when our lives seemed to be going in very different directions," Hailey said of the breakup years later in a February 2020 conversation with ELLE. "I actually think—now that I look back at it being married—that it was a good thing for the two of us, very healthy."

February 10, 2016: They clarify their relationship status.

The two confirm that they are dating—just openly. "We are not an exclusive couple," Hailey tells E! "He's about to go on tour. Relationships at this age are already complicated, but I don't really like to talk about it because it's between me and him."

Justin adds that he's trying not to rush or force things with Hailey. "I don't want to feel like the girl I love is an added responsibility," he tells GQ in a cover interview. "I know that in the past, I've hurt people and said things that I didn't mean to make them happy in the moment. So now I'm just more so looking at the future, making sure I'm not damaging them. What if Hailey ends up being the girl I'm gonna marry, right? If I rush into anything, if I damage her, then it's always gonna be damaged."

December 30, 2015: They go on a tropical vacation.

Rumors of their romance naturally begin to swirl after these photos and videos of Justin and Hailey.

December 27, 2015: They have a romantic dinner in New York.

Justin, now off with Selena, is spotted dining out with Hailey at Mr. Chow in New York. The two are later seen on a movie date, and at a diner in New Jersey the following morning.

Circa 2011: They run into each other at a premiere.

Hailey runs into Justin again at the premiere of his Never Say Never movie. The two reportedly become good friends, though Justin is with Selena Gomez at the time.

Circa 2009: Hailey meets Justin backstage.

A young 12-year-old Hailey meets Justin for the first time as a fan backstage during his appearance on the TODAY show. In uncovered footage from that pivotal day, her famous dad, Stephen Baldwin, introduces her to Justin and she shyly greets him.
