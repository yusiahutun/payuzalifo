    How to Choose a Good Liquid Lipstick?

    Here are a few aspects that you should consider before splurging on the best liquid lipstick: 

    Shade And Skin Tone: Popular liquid lipstick brands have introduced an array of shades. Right from corals, pinks, and nudes to brown, berry red, and maroon, you will find them all in their color palette. These shades more or less look different depending on one’s skin tone and complexion. Light shades like nudes, pinks, and corals look more appealing to those with fair skin tones. Likewise, dark shades like chocolate browns and deep and dark red, look less washed out on those with dark skin tones. 

    To determine your skin’s undertone, take a look at the veins visible on your wrist. If you notice green veins, then you possess a warm undertone. For those with blue or purple veins, it means you have cooler undertones. Lipstick shades that complement warm undertones are earthy reds, vibrant oranges, warm corals, and rich browns. On the other hand, pink, blue, and purple shades are apt for those with cool undertones. The ones with a neutral undertone can choose a mix of both. 

    Key Ingredients: Next, pay heed to the list of key ingredients. To keep your lips moisturized and prevent dehydration and chapped lips, choose lipsticks enriched with lip-nourishing ingredients like vitamin E, jojoba oil, hyaluronic acid, shea butter, coconut oil, beeswax, aloe vera, glycerin, and argan oil. 

    Finish Type: Liquid lipsticks typically possess a semi-matte, matte, soft-matte, and glossy finish. Depending upon your skin tone, comfort level, makeup needs, and preferences, you may pick any.

    Coverage And Pigmentation: Coverage and pigmentation are vital to determine the longevity of the liquid lipstick that you are planning to purchase. A lipstick that is intensely saturated and high in pigmentation will promise to offer full coverage without having the need for reapplication. Typically, a well-pigmented lipstick lasts for up to 16 hours. 

    Texture: Lastly, it is crucial to analyze the texture of the liquid lipstick as it will help you understand its application process better. A lipstick with a creamy, silky smooth, or buttery texture tends to glide effortlessly onto your lips, promising an intense color payoff in a single swipe.

    After taking note of the above-mentioned aspects, below are a few application tips.

    Tips to Apply Liquid Lipstick

    • Begin applying a lip primer to make your lips smooth and look hydrated. 
    • Apply a lip liner that matches your natural lip color or the lipstick shade to contour the edges of your lips for on-point application, with no chances of bleeding or smearing.
    • Follow the lip liner or your natural lip line with the help of the applicator. 
    • Let the lipstick dry completely. Blot your lips using a tissue and remove the excess color. This step will also aid in setting the lip color better.
    • For a more intense color payoff, consider reapplying the lipstick. 

    Tada, you are done!

    FAQ's

    Does liquid lipstick look good on everyone’s lips?

    Yes, liquid lipsticks look good on all skin types and complexions.

    Can liquid lipstick look dry on the lips?

    It depends on the formula of the liquid lipstick. However, a hydrating lipstick with a non-drying formula is a wise investment.

    Are there any tips for making liquid lipstick last longer than usual?

    Yes, you may blot your lipstick with the help of tissue paper to let the color set for a longer time.

    Can liquid lipstick be removed easily?

    Yes, you can remove the liquid lipstick with the help of a makeup remover or eraser.

    Can I use liquid lipstick as a blush?

    Yes, you may use liquid lipstick as a blush by blending it out after applying the lipstick to your cheeks.

    How long does liquid lipstick last?

    Liquid lipstick lasts for up to 6 to 12 months.

    How long does it take for a liquid lipstick to dry?

    It may take up to 5 seconds for a liquid lipstick to dry.
